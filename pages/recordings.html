--- title: Recordings summary: The Brown Band is best known for its scrambling, its skating, and its satire, but few people realize that we are also the only student group to carry on many traditions of Brown that would have otherwise been lost. Today, the Brown Band brings this music out of commencement ceremonies and alumni reunions and into sporting events and campus marches where they can be heard and enjoyed all over Rhode Island. css: [recordings] ---
